Suno Shuffle fixes Suno's built-in shuffle function, which repeats the same songs, skips others entirely, and resets every time you reload.

This extension builds a proper randomised queue from your library and remembers where you left off across page navigations and reloads. Every song plays exactly once before the queue cycles.

Suno often gives uploaded songs the same title. This extension tries to not play songs of the same title in a row, this is integrated into the shuffle function.

To use it, open your Suno library tab (e.g. suno.com/me or suno.com/playlist/liked), then click Start Shuffle in the extension popup. It scans the page, builds the queue, and starts playing immediately via a small floating player.
